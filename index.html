Nyano is a meme about the famous Nyan Cat and the glorious cryptocurrency Nano.

Web & mobile wallets were created to show 1 nyano = 10^-6 XNO (Nano's canonical denomination) to let people play with the feeless digital currency in small amounts, but are now abandoned. You can find the original codebase and some useful references here.
